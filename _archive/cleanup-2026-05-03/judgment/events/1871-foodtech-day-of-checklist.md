## During Event (7:00-7:30 PM Session)

### Engagement Workflow

**1. Hook (15 seconds):**
> "We're Uncle May's Produce, the first data and distribution platform for Black grocery. We're opening a 10,000 sq ft Hyde Park store and building a national rollout model."

**2. Traction (30 seconds):**
> "We're live online selling produce boxes, flagship store in progress, $6.3M Year 1 revenue projection, SBA loan secured."

**3. Ask (15 seconds):**
> "We're raising a $400K-$750K SAFE on a $5M cap. Also looking for Black grocery/food vendors and data partnerships."

**4. Capture:**
> "Can I grab your contact info?" → iPad form or business card exchange

### Lead Capture Process
- [ ] For each conversation, capture lead on iPad form OR exchange business cards
- [ ] Note interest area: Investor / Partner / Vendor / Customer / Press
- [ ] Hand them a teaser sheet to take home
- [ ] Encourage QR code scan for website visit (UTM tracking)
- [ ] If tech fails: write contact info on backup cards

### Key Talking Points (Reference Only)
- NOT a food desert solution — targeting affluent Black communities
- Revenue model: retail + vendor distribution + data insights + platform services
- Team: Booth MBA, Amazon ops, BofA M&A, Unilever/P&G marketing, 2x grocery exits GM
- Proven market: $8M+ existing spend in Hyde Park
- Differentiation: data-first, platform-enabled, heritage-driven

### High-Value Conversations
- [ ] If investor shows strong interest: offer to send full deck + model
- [ ] If vendor/partner: offer follow-up call, share vendor onboarding overview
- [ ] If press: offer interview, share media kit
- [ ] If VIP (check with Jonaie): prioritize extended conversation
