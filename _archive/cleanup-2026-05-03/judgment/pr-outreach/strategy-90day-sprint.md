# Uncle Mays PR Strategy — 90-Day Sprint (Apr 18 → Jul 17, 2026)

> Canonical strategy doc for the PR Director agent. Source of truth for the sprint. Agent should read this every heartbeat and align weekly priorities against the four pillars and phase exit criteria.

## Context

Uncle Mays is shifting from chasing individuals for investor/donor money to an earned-media strategy that pulls media, customers, and capital toward the business. Advisor input (James Beard award winner) recommended this pivot. Zero pitches sent yet — this sprint changes that.

**Anchoring decisions:**
- **Audience:** Dual-track — customers (produce box demand) AND investor halo (coverage visible to raise targets).
- **Hero story:** Produce boxes from Black farmers (concrete, buyable today). Hyde Park flagship is the milestone arc; Anthony is the throughline voice.
- **Advisor role:** Strategic advice only — no network intros or on-record quotes. Plan does NOT depend on his relationships.
- **Tempo:** 90-day sprint, reassess at day 90.
- **Content engine:** Pursue (a) creator-partnership collabs and (b) documentary producer outreach in parallel. Anthony does NOT self-produce. Budget ceiling **$2,000/month** until produce box revenue justifies scaling up.

**One story, two cuts:** "Uncle Mays is building the distribution layer for Black food — starting with boxes that connect Black farmers to households, flagship store next." Customers hear "buy this now"; investors hear "demand is real and the founder is the category voice."

---

## Strategic Spine — Four Pillars

1. **Narrative:** Distribution layer for Black food → produce boxes now → Hyde Park flagship next → Anthony Ivy as the voice.
2. **Proof:** TechRise Award (2025), WBC Food Innovation Award (2025), SXSW 2026 panelist, Booth MBA + PE background, 97% intent-to-shop data, $100B TAM.
3. **Inbound machine:** Every placement drives two destinations — produce box checkout for customers, founder profile for journalists/investors who want to talk.
4. **Creator-amplified owned media:** Partner with existing Black food / Chicago / entrepreneurship creators. In parallel, pitch 3–5 documentary producers to take Uncle Mays as a long-form subject. Owned IG/TikTok output comes from creator collabs, not in-house production.

**Messaging guardrails (already in agent SOUL.md):**
- No "food desert" framing.
- No em dashes in outbound copy.
- All external outreach approved by CEO before send.

## 90-Day Execution

### Phase 1 (Days 1–30 | Apr 18 – May 18): Foundation + quick wins

- **Summit June 18 (Grocery Retail for All):** Register by Jun 1. CEO emails organizers this week. See `summit-opportunity-june-2026.md`.
- **Send Tier 1 pitches:** Ally Marotti / Crain's, Derek Dingle / Black Enterprise (drafts already exist).
- **Customer + farmer story collection:** 3 customers, 2 farmers. B-roll for pitches, op-eds, podcasts.
- **Tier 2 journalist list:** 15 targets across national food (Eater, Civil Eats, Food & Wine), Chicago (Block Club, WTTW, Chicago Reader), Black media (Essence, Root, AfroTech, Capital B).
- **Podcast target list:** 10 shows (Black Tech Green Money, Racist Sandwich, Ground Work, How I Built This, etc.).
- **Draft 2 op-eds:** "Beyond food deserts" and "PE rigor meets grocery." Target Crain's, Tribune, Black Enterprise.
- **Press infrastructure:** `press@unclemays.com`, `/press` page with one-sheet, logos, headshots, award badges.
- **Creator partnership scouting:** 10 Chicago / Black food / entrepreneurship creators (10K–250K followers). Score by fit, engagement, likely cost.
- **Documentary producer scouting:** 5 producers/companies with prior Black food / business / food systems work.

**Phase 1 exit criteria:** 8+ pitches sent · summit slot resolved · 2 op-eds drafted · `/press` live · creator (10) and documentary (5) shortlists CEO-reviewed.

---

### Phase 2 (Days 31–60 | May 18 – Jun 17): Expansion + coverage

- Land 1–2 Tier 1 placements.
- Pitch 8 podcasts, book 3.
- Place 1 op-ed.
- Pitch 3 of 5 documentary producers with tailored "why Uncle Mays" angles. Offer access. Ask: 15-min intro call.
- **Creator collab #1:** Top-3 creator, ≤$1,500. Format: produce-box unboxing / farmer visit / "come along with Anthony." Cross-post to owned channels.
- SXSW post-panel recap, press release, speaker clip reel.
- Apply to 3 fall panels.

**Phase 2 exit criteria:** 1+ investor-read placement · 3 podcast bookings · 1 op-ed placed · summit executed · 1 creator collab shipped · 1+ documentary intro call.

---

### Phase 3 (Days 61–90 | Jun 18 – Jul 17): Momentum + reassess

- Syndicate every win → LinkedIn, newsletter, investor update, pitch bait.
- Second wave pitches using Phase 2 placements as proof.
- Investor halo: share coverage with SAFE holders + pipeline investors.
- **Creator collab #2** if #1 worked (≥3% engagement). If not, redirect $1,500 to paid amplification of earned wins.
- Documentary follow-through: convert intros to scoped proposals (short doc / pilot / sizzle reel). Shelve if no producer bites.
- **Day 90 review:** Heartbeat report → decision memo at `day90-review.md`. CEO + agent decide next 90-day bet.

**Phase 3 exit criteria:** 3–5 placements · measurable inbound · order data correlated to coverage dates · day 90 memo written.
