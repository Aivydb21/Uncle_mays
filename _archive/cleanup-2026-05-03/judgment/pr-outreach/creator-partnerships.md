# Creator Partnership Shortlist

> Goal: Identify 10 creators in the Chicago / Black food / Black entrepreneurship space with audiences that overlap produce-box buyers. Use for paid or barter collabs at ≤$1,500 per collab, 1 to 2 collabs per month, max $2,000/month total content layer budget.
>
> **Status:** Seed list below. PR Director agent should expand to 10 verified entries via Firecrawl + Instagram research and score each on fit, engagement, and likely cost. CEO approves shortlist before any outreach.

## Targeting Criteria

| Criterion | Target |
|---|---|
| Follower range | 10K to 250K (mid-tier — engagement is high, fees are negotiable) |
| Geography | Chicago primary, national Black food/entrepreneurship secondary |
| Audience | Black women 25 to 35, food-curious, conscious-spending |
| Content fit | Cooking, recipes, "day in the life," Black business behind-the-scenes, food sourcing |
| Engagement rate | ≥3% (filter out inflated follower counts) |
| Brand safety | No major controversies, family-friendly, aligned with Black food/wealth narrative |
| Format | Reels + carousel posts, IG primary; TikTok or YouTube Shorts as bonus |

## Collab Format Options

1. **"Come along with Anthony" day-in-the-life** — creator joins Anthony for a farm visit or store build-out tour, captures B-roll, posts a 60-90 sec reel.
2. **Produce box unboxing + cook-along** — creator receives a box, films the unboxing, cooks one recipe with the produce, tags Uncle May's.
3. **Farmer feature** — creator visits a partner farm with Anthony, tells the farmer's story, frames the box as the channel.
4. **Founder Q&A** — creator interviews Anthony for their existing show/channel, Uncle May's story, awards, vision.
