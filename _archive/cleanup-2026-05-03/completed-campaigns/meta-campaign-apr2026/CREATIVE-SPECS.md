## META ADS SPECIFICATIONS

### Image Formats

| Format | Dimensions | Aspect Ratio | Min Resolution | Max File Size | Use Case |
|--------|-----------|--------------|----------------|---------------|----------|
| **Feed (Square)** | 1080 x 1080 px | 1:1 | 600 x 600 px | 30 MB | Instagram/Facebook Feed |
| **Feed (Landscape)** | 1200 x 628 px | 1.91:1 | 600 x 314 px | 30 MB | Facebook Feed (optimized) |
| **Feed (Portrait)** | 1080 x 1350 px | 4:5 | 600 x 750 px | 30 MB | Instagram Feed (tall) |
| **Story/Reel** | 1080 x 1920 px | 9:16 | 600 x 1067 px | 30 MB | Instagram/Facebook Stories & Reels |
| **Carousel** | 1080 x 1080 px | 1:1 | 600 x 600 px | 30 MB each | Multi-image ads (2-10 cards) |

**Recommended Export Settings:**
- File Type: PNG (for text overlays, transparency) or JPG (for photos)
- Color Mode: RGB
- DPI: 72 (web standard)
- Compression: High quality (80-90% JPG, lossless PNG)

### Video Formats

| Format | Dimensions | Aspect Ratio | Duration | Max File Size | Frame Rate | Use Case |
|--------|-----------|--------------|----------|---------------|------------|----------|
| **Feed Video** | 1080 x 1080 px | 1:1 | 1s - 60s | 4 GB | 30 fps min | Instagram/Facebook Feed |
| **Feed Video (Landscape)** | 1280 x 720 px | 16:9 | 1s - 60s | 4 GB | 30 fps min | Facebook Feed (wide) |
| **Story/Reel Video** | 1080 x 1920 px | 9:16 | 1s - 90s | 4 GB | 30 fps min | Instagram/Facebook Stories & Reels |
| **Reel (Short)** | 1080 x 1920 px | 9:16 | 1s - 60s | 4 GB | 30 fps min | Instagram Reels (optimized) |

**Video Specs:**
- Codec: H.264 or H.265
- Audio: AAC, 128kbps min
- Bitrate: Variable (VBR recommended)
- Captions: Burned-in or SRT file (85% watch with sound off)

**Meta Video Requirements:**
- First 3 seconds MUST hook (autoplay with no sound)
- Include captions for all spoken content
- Add visual brand elements (logo, colors) early
- End with clear CTA (text overlay + voiceover)

---

### Carousel Specifications

**Card Count:** 2-10 cards per carousel  
**Image Dimensions:** 1080 x 1080 px (square, all cards must match)  
**Video Dimensions:** 1080 x 1080 px (can mix images + videos)  
**Max File Size:** 30 MB per card  

**Carousel Best Practices:**
- Card 1: Hook (stop the scroll)
- Cards 2-4: Product/benefit showcase
- Final Card: CTA (price, offer, action)
- All cards should tell a story (sequential, not random)

---

### Text Overlay Guidelines (Meta)

**On-Image Text:**
- Keep text to <20% of image area (Meta no longer enforces, but performs better)
- Use high-contrast text (white on dark, dark on light)
- Font size: Large enough to read on mobile (headline 60pt+, body 30pt+)
- Avoid thin fonts (hard to read on small screens)

**Ad Copy Character Limits:**
- **Primary Text:** 125 characters (displays fully on mobile)
- **Headline:** 40 characters (appears below image)
- **Description:** 30 characters (not always shown)
- **CTA Button:** Pre-set options (Shop Now, Learn More, etc.)

**Note:** Text can be longer, but will be truncated with "... See More" on mobile. Keep critical info in first 125 chars.

## GOOGLE ADS SPECIFICATIONS

### Search Ads (Text Only)

| Element | Character Limit | Count | Notes |
|---------|----------------|-------|-------|
| **Headlines** | 30 characters | 3 per ad | All 3 rotate, may not all show |
| **Descriptions** | 90 characters | 2 per ad | Both rotate, may not all show |
| **Display Path** | 15 characters | 2 paths | Appears in green URL (optional) |
| **Final URL** | N/A | 1 | Actual landing page URL |

**Recommended Character Usage:**
- Use full 30 chars for headlines (more real estate)
- Front-load descriptions (critical info first 50 chars)
- Include keyword in Headline 1 for relevance

**YouTube Video Specs:**
- Codec: H.264
- Frame Rate: 24-30 fps
- Audio: Stereo AAC, 128kbps
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9 (landscape) or 9:16 (vertical, Shorts)

**YouTube Best Practices:**
- First 5 seconds: Hook before skip button appears
- Brand early: Logo/brand name in first 3s
- Captions: Always include (accessibility + sound-off viewers)
- CTA: End card with clear action

## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC NOTES

### Instagram Reels
- **Optimal Duration:** 7-15 seconds (higher completion rate)
- **Hook Window:** First 1-2 seconds (fastest scroll speed)
- **Music:** Trending audio increases reach (optional for ads)
- **Captions:** Always on (85% watch with sound off)

### Facebook Feed
- **Image vs. Video:** Video gets 2-3x engagement, but higher production cost
- **Carousel Performance:** Carousel ads get 10x engagement vs. single image (use for storytelling)
- **Text Overlay:** More text = lower reach (keep <20% of image area)

### Instagram Stories
- **Swipe-Up CTA:** Only available for ads (organic needs 10k followers)
- **Interactive Elements:** Polls, questions increase engagement (organic only, not ads)
- **Lifetime:** Stories disappear after 24h (organic), ads stay as long as budget runs

### Google Search
- **Quality Score:** Impacts CPC (relevance, landing page experience, CTR)
- **Ad Rank:** Bid × Quality Score = position
- **Extensions:** Add sitelinks, callouts, structured snippets for more real estate

### Google Performance Max
- **Asset Diversity:** More assets = better performance (15 headlines > 3 headlines)
- **Creative Rotation:** Google tests all combos, surfaces best performers
- **Patience Required:** Needs 2-4 weeks to learn and optimize
