## Sequence Notes

**Voice:** First person, founder. Never "they" — always "I" and "we."

**CTA pattern:**
- Email 1: "Worth a conversation?" (low friction, reply-based)
- Email 2: "Happy to share the deck or model" (offer value)
- Email 3: "I'd welcome 20 minutes" (specific but not presumptuous)
- Email 4: "If this doesn't fit, no worries" (permission to say no — counterintuitively increases replies)

**Attachments:** Only attach teaser on Email 1. Offer deck/model in Emails 2-3 as a reply trigger.

**Calendly:** Do NOT include in cold emails. Send scheduling link only after someone replies expressing interest.

**Subject lines:** Lowercase, conversational, no dollar signs or exclamation marks. Test one per campaign and measure open rates.

**Personalization:** Where possible, add a line in Email 1 referencing the recipient's fund thesis, a recent investment they made, or their sector focus. Even one sentence of personalization dramatically increases open and reply rates.
