## Copy Principles Applied

All copy follows brand voice guidelines:

✅ **No em dashes** (use commas, periods, colons instead)  
✅ **Active voice** ("We deliver" not "Produce is delivered")  
✅ **Specificity wins** ("Collards, okra, sweet potatoes" beats "fresh vegetables")  
✅ **Confident, not defensive** (premium positioning, no apologies for pricing)  
✅ **No food desert framing** (avoid charity messaging, focus on quality + culture)  
✅ **Mobile-optimized** (short sentences, punchy hooks, fast to read)

## UTM Parameter Reference

All URLs use consistent UTM tagging per RevOps standards (see `marketing-launch-tracking-deliverables.md`):

**Meta:**
- `utm_source`: `facebook` or `instagram`
- `utm_medium`: `paid_social` (Feed) or `paid_social_stories` (Stories/Reels)
- `utm_campaign`: `conversion_apr21`
- `utm_content`: `{hook}_video_{variant_number}` (e.g., `quality_video_1`)
- `promo`: `FREESHIP`

**Google Ads:**
- `utm_source`: `google`
- `utm_medium`: `video` (YouTube) or `pmax` (Performance Max)
- `utm_campaign`: `conversion_apr21`
- `utm_content`: `{placement}_1` (e.g., `youtube_bumper_1`, `pmax_video_1`)
- `promo`: `FREESHIP`
