## Brand Voice Guardrails

✅ **DO:**
- Speak naturally, like talking to a friend
- Mention specific produce (greens, okra, yams, sweet potatoes)
- Emphasize convenience and quality
- Reference cultural connection ("my grandma's greens", "food I grew up with")
- Show genuine enthusiasm

❌ **DON'T:**
- Sound scripted or rehearsed (use scripts as guides, not verbatim)
- Use charity language ("helping communities", "food desert solution")
- Oversell or make exaggerated claims
- Apologize for the price (we're premium, own it)

## Video 1: Value + Quality Hook
**Target Hook:** "I used to spend $200/week at Whole Foods. Now I get Uncle May's for $55 and the produce is actually fresh."  
**Objective:** Reframe pricing as value (quality + cost savings)  
**Length:** 20-25 seconds

### Script

**[0-3sec] HOOK (must grab attention immediately):**  
*[Close-up on face, direct to camera]*  
"I was spending $200 a week at Whole Foods."

**[3-10sec] PROBLEM:**  
*[Cut to casual setting, holding produce or unpacking box]*  
"And half the greens would be wilted by the time I got home. I was paying for convenience, not quality."

**[10-20sec] SOLUTION:**  
*[Show Uncle May's box, pick up greens]*  
"Now I get Uncle May's. $55 a week. Delivered every Wednesday. And the produce? Actually fresh. Like, my-grandma's-garden fresh."

**[20-25sec] CTA:**  
*[Direct to camera, confident]*  
"Subscribe at unclemays.com. Use code FREESHIP for $10 off your first box."

### Shot List
1. Close-up (face, direct address) — 0-3sec
2. Medium shot (unpacking box or holding produce) — 3-10sec
3. Close-up (greens in hand, showing quality) — 10-20sec
4. Close-up (face, CTA delivery) — 20-25sec

### Props Needed
- Uncle May's produce box (or any box with fresh greens)
- Collard greens or kale (hero item)

## Production Notes

### Filming Tips for Anthony (If Placeholder Testimonials)

1. **Shoot 3-5 takes of each script** — pick the most natural delivery
2. **Don't memorize verbatim** — use scripts as guide, speak naturally
3. **Film in natural light** — near window, outdoors, or well-lit kitchen
4. **Hold phone vertically** — 9:16 aspect ratio (Instagram/TikTok format)
5. **Keep it simple** — one location, minimal setup, focus on authenticity
6. **Audio matters** — quiet space, no background TV/music
7. **Energy level** — conversational but enthusiastic (excited friend, not salesperson)

### If Filming with Actual Customers

1. **Incentive:** $25 credit per video (offer to 10-15 existing customers via Mailchimp)
2. **Send them the scripts** but encourage their own words
3. **Key talking points to hit:**
   - What they love about the produce (specifics: greens, okra, yams)
   - Why they subscribed (convenience, quality, Black-owned)
   - Who it's for (their family, their community)
4. **Collect via email** — customers film on phone, send MP4 via WeTransfer or Google Drive
5. **Review for brand safety** — ensure no charity language, no off-brand messaging
