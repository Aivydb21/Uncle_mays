## Risks & Mitigations

**Risk: Checkout still broken**
- Mitigation: CTO deployed fix 1 hour ago, monitoring for first successful order
- Fallback: If no successful orders in 24h, pause campaign and investigate

**Risk: Insufficient traffic to hit 10-15 orders**

**Risk: Promo code abuse (multiple uses)**
- Mitigation: Stripe promo codes can be single-use per customer
- Monitor: Track redemptions daily

**Risk: Can't fulfill 15+ orders on short notice**
- Mitigation: Coordinate with COO on inventory/delivery capacity
- Confirm: 15 boxes is well within current fulfillment capacity
