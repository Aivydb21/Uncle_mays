# LANDING PAGE REQUIREMENTS

For both Search and Performance Max campaigns, the landing page MUST include:

1. **Message Match:** Headline matches ad promise (e.g., "Fresh Produce Delivered to Hyde Park")
2. **Clear CTA:** "Order Your Box" button above the fold
3. **Price Transparency:** Show box price ($30-50 range) prominently
4. **No Subscription Surprise:** State "No commitment required" or "One-time order available"
5. **Social Proof:** 89% referral stat, customer testimonials
6. **Mobile Optimized:** Most Google traffic is mobile
7. **Fast Load:** <2s load time (critical for Quality Score and conversion)

# TESTING STRATEGY

## Week 1: Keyword Testing (Search)

- Google auto-tests all headline/description combos
