# Meta Ad Copy: 15 Variants for Customer Acquisition Campaign

**Campaign:** 30 Orders/Week Customer Acquisition  
**Owner:** Advertising Creative  
**Status:** Ready for CRO Review  
**Date:** 2026-04-14

---

## Brand Guidelines (All Variants)

- **Tone:** Premium but warm, investor-to-investor (not corporate, not overly casual)
- **NO food desert framing, NO poverty porn, NO "saving the community" messaging**
- **Positioning:** Curated produce for Black communities with higher economic capacity
- **Target:** Black women, ages 25-35, Hyde Park Chicago + surrounding neighborhoods
- **Meta Character Limits:** Headline ≤40 chars, Primary Text ≤125 chars
