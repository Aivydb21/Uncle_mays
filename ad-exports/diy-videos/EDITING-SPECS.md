### Caption Track (full video)
> "Look at this quality. Farm-fresh, delivered to Hyde Park. This is what real produce looks like. Uncle May's. Starting at thirty dollars. Free delivery on your first box at unclemays.com."

**Caption style:** White text, black semi-transparent background pill, 85% screen width, bottom 15% of frame. Auto-break at natural pauses.

### Edit Notes
- Cut to beat if background music added (optional: upbeat lo-fi, no vocals)
- Close-up inserts on greens/okra highly encouraged (B-roll from midjourney-generated/ can be used as static insert if needed)
- Keep pace brisk — unboxing reveal before 10-second mark
- End on product/box clearly visible, not just person

### Export Settings
- Resolution: 1080x1920
- Codec: H.264 (AAC audio if music)
- Bitrate: 4–8 Mbps
- Max file size: 500MB (Meta requirement)
