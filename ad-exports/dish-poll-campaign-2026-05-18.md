## 1. Five Dish Concepts (Black-Chicago Sunday Table)

Every dish below is built from SKUs we already sell on the live catalog (Airtable base `appm6F6H9obydzAM2`, table `Catalog`). No dish requires a SKU we cannot fulfill.

### Dish 1. Smothered Short Ribs over Sweet Potato Mash

Braised grass-fed beef short ribs, slow-cooked with candy carrots and a deep onion gravy, plated over garlic-smashed sweet potatoes. Sunday-supper centerpiece. Two-hour cook, mostly hands-off.

Catalog ingredients pulled: beef short ribs (bone-in $8.50/lb or boneless $12.50/lb), sweet potatoes ($2.50 ea), candy orange carrots ($1.50 / 0.5 lb).
Kit add-on: aromatics + braise spice pouch.

### Dish 2. Pembroke Greens & Beans (Vegan)

Tuscan kale slow-cooked low and slow with smoked paprika and garlic, ladled over creamy stewed pinto beans, finished with a candy-carrot quick pickle. The argument for "you don't need a ham hock to make greens sing." Vegan, gluten-free, freezer-friendly.

Catalog ingredients pulled: Tuscan kale ($3.00), pinto beans 1 lb ($6.00 range), candy orange carrots ($1.50).
Kit add-on: smoke + garlic spice pouch, vinegar packet for the pickle.

### Dish 3. Black Bean & Sweet Potato Chili

Hyde Park weeknight chili. Black turtle beans, charred sweet potato cubes, candy carrots, slow-built tomato-free Mexican-Mississippi delta hybrid. Doubles down on Tuesday-night meal prep.

Catalog ingredients pulled: black turtle beans 1/2 lb sampler ($3.50) or 1 lb ($6.00 range), 2 sweet potatoes ($2.50 ea), candy carrots ($1.50).
Kit add-on: chili spice blend.

### Dish 4. Lamb Chops & Pea-Shoot Salad

Date-night plate. Pan-seared lamb chops with a quick pea-shoot + radish-microgreen salad, candy carrots roasted with honey on the side. Twelve-minute cook. Wine-pairing photogenic.

Catalog ingredients pulled: lamb chops $10/pair, pea shoots clamshell $5.00, candy carrots $1.50, optional purple radish microgreens $5.00.
Kit add-on: finishing oil + spice rub.

### Dish 5. Auntie's One-Pan Chicken, Kale & Sweet Potato

Whole pastured chicken (or jointed thighs) roasted over a bed of sweet potatoes and kale, drippings basting the greens. Single sheet pan. Kid-friendly. The "you can cook this on a Tuesday and still have leftovers Thursday" story.

Catalog ingredients pulled: whole chicken ($32) or chicken thighs, 2 sweet potatoes, 1 bunch Tuscan kale, candy carrots.
Kit add-on: lemon-herb roast rub.

## 3. Visual Asset — Hero "Vote Board"

**No Canva.** Two acceptable paths, in order of preference.

### Path A (preferred): Chef-shot overhead "vote board"

A single overhead photograph, 1080×1080, of all five dishes' raw ingredients arranged on a butcher-paper or dark wood surface. Each cluster has a hand-lettered numbered card (1–5) with the dish name on a small kraft tag. Natural window light. Phone camera is fine.

**Why this wins:** It tells the brand story (real food, real chef, real farm) in one frame, beats any AI-generated mockup, and is unmistakably not a stock template. The "vote board" reads instantly when someone scrolls.

**Shot list for chef:**
- Cluster 1: 1 lb beef short ribs (bone-in, on butcher paper) + 1 sweet potato + a few candy carrots. Card: "1 Short Ribs + Sweet Potato Mash"
- Cluster 2: 1 bunch Tuscan kale (un-stripped) + a small ramekin of pinto beans + 2–3 candy carrots. Card: "2 Pembroke Greens & Beans (vegan)"
- Cluster 3: A small dish of dry black turtle beans + 1 sweet potato + a couple candy carrots. Card: "3 Black Bean Sweet Potato Chili"
- Cluster 4: 2 lamb chops on a wooden board + a pea-shoot clamshell (open) + 2 candy carrots. Card: "4 Lamb Chops & Pea Shoots"
- Cluster 5: Whole chicken or thigh quarters + 2 sweet potatoes + 1 bunch kale + candy carrots fanned. Card: "5 One-Pan Chicken, Kale, Sweet Potato"

Add a sixth visible element: a chalkboard or kraft sign at the top reading **"Which one cooks first? Vote in the comments."**

Crop: 1:1 square (1080×1080) for IG feed + FB. If chef wants to shoot 4:5 portrait for IG too, that is welcome as a second crop, but 1:1 cross-posts the cleanest.
