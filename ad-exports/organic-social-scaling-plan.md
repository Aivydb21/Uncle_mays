## What Worked (Reverse-Engineered)

Based on the Apr 14 and Apr 16 content that preceded the spike:

1. **Community identity messaging** — "For us, by us" and "named for my great-grandmother" resonated strongly
2. **Founder story format** — Personal narrative + mission outperformed product-only posts
3. **Direct call to action** — "unclemays.com/checkout" in post drove direct checkout traffic
4. **Facebook community group sharing** — The Apr 20-21 spike pattern (exponential, mostly mobile Facebook) indicates posts were shared into Hyde Park / South Side community groups
5. **Pricing transparency** — All 3 box sizes listed with prices in post copy removed friction
6. **Cultural positioning** — "Black farmers" + "Hyde Park" + "community" language drove shares

### Content Formula That Works
```
Hook (identity/story) + Mission (why this matters) + Product (what you get) + Price + CTA
```

Posts that led with product alone (scarcity post) drove fewer sessions than those that led with identity/story.

## Scaling Strategy: 4 Posts/Week on Facebook

**Previous cadence:** 2 posts/week (Tue/Fri)
**New cadence:** 4 posts/week (Mon/Wed/Fri/Sun)

### Why 4x/week:
- Facebook algorithm rewards consistent posting (3-5x/week is optimal for organic reach)
- Each post has a 48-72h shelf life before reach drops
- More posts = more opportunities for community group re-shares
- Weekend posts (Sun) capture different audience segments

### Content Mix (Weekly Rotation)

| Day | Content Type | Purpose | Expected Engagement |
|-----|-------------|---------|-------------------|
| **Monday** | Founder Story / Behind-the-Scenes | Build trust, drive shares | High shares |
| **Wednesday** | What's In The Box This Week | Drive orders, product showcase | High clicks |
| **Friday** | Community / Customer Proof | Social validation, identity | High saves/shares |
| **Sunday** | Weekend Special / Limited Availability | FOMO, urgency | High clicks |

## Distribution Amplifiers

### Facebook Community Groups (FREE, highest ROI)
This is likely what caused the Apr 20-21 spike. Anthony or team should share posts to:
- Hyde Park Herald / Hyde Park community groups
- South Side Chicago groups
- Black Chicago community groups
- Black-owned business support groups
- Local food / farmers market groups

**Rule:** Share the post itself (not a link), add a 1-line personal comment when sharing. Do not spam. 2-3 group shares per post maximum.

### Instagram Strategy (Secondary)
Instagram drove only 41 sessions vs Facebook's 1,367. It's not the priority, but maintain presence:
- Cross-post all Facebook content to Instagram
- Use Stories with link sticker for each post
- Reels are optional but high-potential if Anthony can do 15-30 second videos

### Engagement Protocol
- Respond to ALL Facebook comments within 4 hours (algorithm rewards active threads)
- DM anyone who asks "how do I order?" with direct checkout link
- Like/react to every comment (even negative ones get a thoughtful response)
- Save positive comments for future testimonial posts

## Conversion Gap (Flag for CRO)

**Problem:** 98 organic social sessions landed directly on checkout pages with 0 conversions. 1,413 total organic social sessions produced only 1 conversion.

**Possible causes:**
1. Checkout page friction (form too long, payment issues, unclear pricing)
2. No product education on checkout page (jumps straight to payment)
3. Missing trust signals on checkout page
4. Mobile experience issues (96.7% of traffic is Facebook, heavily mobile)

**Recommendation:** CRO should audit the /checkout/family and /checkout/starter mobile experience immediately. Organic social is delivering the traffic. The conversion funnel is losing it.
