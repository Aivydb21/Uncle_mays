## Copy Testing Strategy

**A/B Test Variables:**
- **Hook type:** Social proof (V1, V5) vs Cultural (V2) vs Direct offer (V3, V7) vs Problem/solution (V4)
- **CTA:** "Shop Now" vs "Get 20% Off" vs "Claim Discount" vs "Save 20% Now" vs "Try It Now"
- **Price framing:** "$24" vs "$24 (reg $30)" vs "Save $6" vs "20% off"
- **Urgency:** "Today" vs "This week" vs "Tuesday" vs none

**Recommended First Test:**
- Run V1 (social proof), V2 (cultural), and V3 (direct offer) against each other
- Story + Feed formats
- Monitor CTR and CPC for first 48 hours
- Double down on winner, iterate losers

## Creative Execution Notes

**Conversion Crisis Context:**
- 97% checkout abandonment = major trust/friction issue
- Copy must explicitly address: trust (social proof), value (price clarity), ease (no commitment)
- Avoid vague language, use concrete details: "$24", "Hyde Park", "this week"
- Every variant includes clear next step and price point

**Virality Elements:**
- V2 and V5 have highest shareability (cultural resonance + curiosity)
- V1 leverages proven stat (89% would refer) for social validation
- V4 has meme potential ("Tired of grocery runs?")

**Local Targeting:**
- All variants include "Hyde Park" for geographic specificity
- Can adjust in targeting settings vs copy (recommend both for trust)
