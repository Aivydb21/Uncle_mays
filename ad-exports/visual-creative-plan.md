## VISUAL STRATEGY NOTES

**What's Working (Based on Existing Assets):**
- Clean, modern design aesthetic
- Bold typography
- Product photography (fresh produce)
- Green/earth tones consistent with produce/freshness
- "For us, by us" cultural messaging
- Social proof stats prominently featured

**Adjustments for 20% Off Campaign:**
- Add urgency/scarcity visual cues (badges, limited time callouts)
- Price prominently displayed (before/after or savings amount)
- Clear CTA buttons ("Shop Now", "Get 20% Off")
- Maintain brand consistency while emphasizing offer

**Conversion Optimization:**
- Show actual product (builds trust vs abstract concepts)
- Include price (removes friction/surprise at checkout)
- Use "20% off" AND "$24" (test which resonates more)
- Social proof badges ("89% recommend", "Hyde Park's choice")
