# Video Concept Brief: Retargeting — "You Were This Close"
**Type:** UGC-style unboxing / testimonial hybrid  
**Duration:** 15-30 seconds  
**Platform:** Meta (Instagram Reels, Facebook In-Feed Video, Stories)  
**Audience:** Retargeting (bounced site visitors, past 30 days)  
**Parent Issue:** UNC-572

---

## Concept

**Title:** "You Were This Close"

**Core idea:** A real customer (or founder-as-proxy) unboxes an Uncle May's produce box on camera, reacting to the quality of each item. The video opens with a direct-address hook acknowledging the viewer has already visited the site. It closes with the 20% off offer and a clear CTA.

**Why this works for retargeting:**
- Direct address ("You already checked us out") creates recognition and breaks the fourth wall
- UGC-style production signals authenticity, not corporate polish
- Unboxing format satisfies the "what am I actually getting?" objection
- Testimonial voice builds trust through identification, not brand claims
- Short-form format matches platform-native behavior on Reels/Stories

---

## Script

### Version A: Founder Voice (Anthony)

**[0-3s] HOOK (pattern interrupt)**  
*Camera: Close-up of hands holding a closed Uncle May's box, kitchen counter background*  
**VO:** "You visited Uncle May's. You looked at the box. But you didn't order."

**[3-8s] TENSION**  
*Camera: Hands open the box, camera tilts down to reveal contents*  
**VO:** "Let me show you what you almost had."

**[8-18s] PRODUCT REVEAL (the money shot)**  
*Camera: Slow pull of each item, held up to camera*  
- "These collard greens? Fresh from this week." *(holds up greens)*
- "Okra. Real okra." *(holds up okra)*  
- "Sweet potatoes, heirloom tomatoes, fresh herbs." *(quick cuts between items)*

**[18-23s] SOCIAL PROOF**  
*Camera: Back to founder face, warm smile*  
**VO:** "89% of our customers refer their friends. That's not marketing. That's produce."

**[23-30s] OFFER + CTA**  
*Camera: Box in frame, text overlay appears*  
**VO:** "First box, 20% off. $24. No subscription required."  
**Text overlay:** "$24 FIRST BOX — 20% OFF" + "TAP TO ORDER" + Uncle May's logo  
**End card:** unclemays.com with promo badge

## Production Specs

### Format Requirements
| Platform | Dimensions | Duration | Safe Zone |
|----------|-----------|----------|-----------|
| Instagram Reels | 1080x1920 | 15-30s | Text in center 80% |
| Facebook In-Feed | 1080x1080 or 1080x1350 | 15-30s | Text in center 90% |
| Stories | 1080x1920 | 15s max per card | Top/bottom 15% clear for UI |

### Shooting Requirements
- **Camera:** iPhone 13+ (4K, or 1080p minimum)
- **Orientation:** Vertical (9:16) for Reels/Stories, square crop for feed
- **Lighting:** Natural daylight preferred (near window), no harsh overhead
- **Audio:** Built-in mic acceptable for UGC feel, but clear audio essential
- **Background:** Clean kitchen counter, home setting (NOT studio — UGC authenticity)
- **Wardrobe:** Casual, relatable (no branding unless organic)

### Editing Requirements
- Quick cuts between product reveals (1-2 seconds per item)
- Text overlays: bold sans-serif, high contrast (white on dark, or green badge)
- Captions: Burned-in subtitles for silent autoplay (85% of Meta video is watched without sound)
- End card: 3-5 seconds with offer, CTA, and logo
- Music: Optional trending audio or light background beat (check licensing)

### Text Overlay Specs
- **Hook text (0-3s):** 48pt bold, white, center screen
- **Product labels (8-18s):** 36pt, white with dark shadow, lower third
- **Offer text (23-30s):** 64pt bold, green badge background, center
- **CTA (23-30s):** 40pt, button style, bottom third
- **Logo:** 80px, bottom-right corner throughout
