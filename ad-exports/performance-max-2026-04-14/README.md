## ⚠️ Production Blockers

**Current Tool Limitations:**
- No AI image generation capability
- No video synthesis/editing capability
- No access to product photography

**Recommended Resolution Paths:**

### Option 1: External Production (Fastest)
- Hire freelance photographer + videographer
- Timeline: 7 days (booking → shoot → edit → delivery)
- Cost: $2,350-$5,300 total
- **Pros:** Highest quality, authentic representation, full creative control
- **Cons:** Higher cost, requires talent/location coordination

### Option 2: AI Tools (Lower Cost)
- Subscribe to AI image generation (Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion)
- Use stock footage + motion graphics for video
- Timeline: 3-5 days (learning curve + production)
- Cost: $500-$1,500 total
- **Pros:** Lower cost, faster iteration, no talent needed
- **Cons:** May lack cultural authenticity, limited video quality

### Option 3: Hybrid Approach (Recommended)
- AI image generation for display ads
- External production for 1-2 hero videos
- Timeline: 5-7 days
- Cost: $1,000-$2,500
- **Pros:** Balance of cost/quality, allows testing both approaches
- **Cons:** Still requires external video production
