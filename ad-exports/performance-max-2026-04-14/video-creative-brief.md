## Format-Specific Adaptations

### Landscape (16:9) - YouTube, Display, Discovery
- Use full widescreen composition
- Text safe zones: avoid bottom 20% (where UI overlays appear)
- Logo in top-right or bottom-right corner during mid-video
- Suitable for longer watch time (45-60 seconds)

### Vertical (9:16) - YouTube Shorts, Instagram Stories, Mobile
- Reframe all shots for vertical composition
- Keep subject centered vertically
- Text overlays: top third or bottom third (avoid center where faces will be)
- Optimize for sound-off viewing (text must carry the message)
- Faster pacing preferred (30-40 seconds ideal)

## Production Guidelines

**Casting (if talent needed):**
- Primary subject: Black woman, ages 25-35
- Authentic, natural performance (not overly scripted)
- Comfortable on camera, genuine expressions
- Hyde Park or Chicago-based talent preferred (local authenticity)

**Locations:**
- Hyde Park residential street (establishing shots)
- Modern, relatable kitchen (not overly staged or luxury)
- Optional: Local produce source or farmer's market (if "farm to door" narrative)

**Cinematography:**
- Natural lighting preferred (golden hour for outdoor, bright indirect for indoor)
- Handheld or gimbal for dynamic movement (avoid static tripod unless intentional)
- Shallow depth of field for product close-ups (f/2.8 or lower)
- 4K capture preferred (deliver 1080p for file size management)

**Color Grading:**
- Warm, vibrant, saturated (but not over-processed)
- Emphasize greens, reds, yellows of fresh produce
- Consistent color temperature across all shots
- Avoid overly cool or sterile tones

**Audio:**
- Professional voiceover (warm, authentic, not "announcer" voice)
- Music: licensed or royalty-free (no copyright issues)
- Sound effects: subtle (box opening, produce sounds, kitchen ambiance)
- Mix voiceover at -16 LUFS, music at -20 LUFS (background)

**Text Overlays:**
- Bold sans-serif font (Arial Bold, Helvetica Bold, or similar)
- White text with black drop shadow or solid background
- Animated entrance/exit (subtle: fade, slide, not distracting)
- On-screen duration: 2-4 seconds per text card
- Font size: Large enough for mobile viewing (min 72pt)

## Google Ads Performance Max Video Best Practices

- **First 5 seconds are critical:** Hook must grab attention immediately
- **Sound-off optimization:** 80% of mobile users watch without sound; text overlays must tell the story
- **Branding:** Logo should appear within first 5 seconds or last 5 seconds
- **Call to Action:** Clear, direct CTA in final 5-10 seconds
- **Aspect ratios:** Both 16:9 and 9:16 are required for full Performance Max coverage
- **Mobile-first:** Design for vertical viewing (9:16 will get more impressions)
