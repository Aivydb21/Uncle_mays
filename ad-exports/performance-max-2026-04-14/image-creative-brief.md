## Typography & Layout Specs

**Headline Typography:**
- Font: Bold sans-serif (Arial Bold, Helvetica Bold, or similar)
- Size: 60-80pt (scaled for each format)
- Color: White with 50% black drop shadow, OR solid black on light backgrounds
- Placement: Top third or center (never bottom)

**Subtext Typography:**
- Font: Regular or medium sans-serif
- Size: 30-40pt
- Color: Same treatment as headline
- Placement: Below headline, 20-30px spacing

**CTA Button:**
- Style: Rounded rectangle, solid color (brand green or contrasting accent)
- Typography: Bold sans-serif, white text
- Size: 40-50pt
- Placement: Bottom third, centered or aligned with text

**Logo:**
- Size: 120-150px square (proportional to image size)
- Placement: Corner (non-intrusive)
- Background: Transparent or subtle white/black backing if contrast needed

---

## Photography Direction

**Lighting:**
- Natural light preferred (golden hour or bright indirect daylight)
- Avoid harsh shadows or overexposure
- Consistent color temperature across all images (5500-6500K)

**Styling:**
- Fresh, clean, uncluttered
- Avoid overly staged or artificial aesthetics
- Emphasize vibrant color and texture of produce
- Include variety (not just one type of vegetable)

**Cultural Authenticity:**
- If featuring people, ensure authentic representation of target demographic (Black women, 25-35)
- Avoid stock photo clichés
- Kitchen/home settings should feel real and relatable, not sterile

**Brand Consistency:**
- All images should feel cohesive (similar color grading, lighting style)
- Uncle May's logo should be consistent size/placement across variants
- Text overlay style should be uniform

## Notes for Production

- Google Ads Performance Max will auto-crop and resize these images for various placements (Search, Display, YouTube, Discover)
- Keep critical visual elements (face, logo, headline) within "safe zones" (center 80% of image)
- Avoid placing text too close to edges (min 50px margin)
- Test images against both light and dark backgrounds
- Ensure logo has sufficient contrast against background in all variants
