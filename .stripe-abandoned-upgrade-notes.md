## Stripe Abandoned Checkout - 3-Email Sequence Upgrade

### Changes in This Version

**Phase 1A → Phase 1B:** Extended from 1-email to 3-email sequence

1. **New tracking system:**
   - Changed from simple Set to EmailTracker map
   - Tracks which specific emails (1, 2, or 3) were sent per session
   - File: `.stripe-abandoned-emails.json`

2. **3-email sequence:**
   - Email 1: 1-24 hours after abandonment (cart reminder)
   - Email 2: 24-48 hours after abandonment (urgency + deadline)
   - Email 3: 48-72 hours after abandonment (final scarcity)

3. **Email content:**
   - All copy from Advertising Creative (`ad-exports/abandoned-checkout-email-sequence.md`)
   - UTM tracking per email (`recovery_email1`, `recovery_email2`, `recovery_email3`)
   - Escalating urgency and visual design (red CTA in Email 3)

4. **Logic:**
   - Email 1 requires: No Email 1 sent yet
   - Email 2 requires: Email 1 sent, Email 2 not sent
   - Email 3 requires: Email 2 sent, Email 3 not sent
