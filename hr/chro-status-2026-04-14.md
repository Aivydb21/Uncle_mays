## Risks and Mitigation

### Risk 1: Funding Delay
**Impact:** Hiring timeline pushed back, store opening delayed  
**Mitigation:**
- All hiring materials ready to launch immediately
- JDs, wage bands, timeline already approved
- Matt Weschler (GM) confirmed and warm
- Can activate hiring within 24 hours of funding close

### Risk 2: Low Application Volume
**Impact:** Can't fill 11 roles in 16-week timeline  
**Mitigation:**
- Multi-channel sourcing: Indeed, LinkedIn, community events, culinary schools, referrals
- Competitive wages (10-30% above market)
- Mission-driven employer brand (Uncle May's is building something historic)
- Employee referral bonuses ($100 per successful hire)
- Contingency: Hiring events, sign-on bonuses if needed

### Risk 3: Key Hire Declines Offer
**Impact:** Timeline delay, scramble for backup  
**Mitigation:**
- Always maintain 2-3 backup candidates for leadership roles
- Move to backup within 48 hours of decline
- 1-week buffer built into timeline for unexpected delays

### Risk 4: High Early Turnover
**Impact:** Operational disruption, re-hiring costs  
**Mitigation:**
- Hire 1 extra cashier (6 instead of 5) as buffer
- Strong onboarding program (30-day and 90-day reviews catch issues early)
- Competitive wages and mission-driven culture attract long-term talent
- Maintain active candidate pipeline for 60 days post-opening
