### Front-End Team

#### 5-10. Cashiers / Customer Service (4-6 FTE)
**Status:** To be hired  
**Reports to:** Assistant Manager

## Labor Model Assumptions

**Average hours per week:**
- GM: 50 hours (salary, no OT)
- Asst Manager: 45 hours (salary, no OT)
- Department Leads (2): 40 hours each (salary or hourly, pending wage research)
- Cashiers (5): 25-35 hours each = 125-175 hours total
- Stock Lead: 40 hours
- Stock Associates (2): 30 hours each = 60 hours

**Total weekly labor hours:** 420-480 hours  
**Labor as % of revenue:** Target 12-15% (industry benchmark: 10-15%)

At $6.3M annual revenue = $121K/week  
Target labor cost: $14,500-18,000/week = $754K-936K annually

## Key Risks

1. **GM dependency** - Matt Weschler is the only confirmed hire. If he becomes unavailable, the entire hiring timeline collapses. **Mitigation:** Maintain backup GM candidates.

2. **Wage competition** - Whole Foods and Pete's Fresh Market may be hiring simultaneously in Chicago. **Mitigation:** Competitive wage research and faster offer timelines.

3. **Training time** - 2-3 week training periods for leads mean we need to start hiring 4-6 weeks before store opening. **Mitigation:** Map hiring timeline to construction schedule.

4. **Part-time cashier reliability** - High turnover in part-time retail roles. **Mitigation:** Hire 1 extra cashier as buffer, invest in onboarding quality.
