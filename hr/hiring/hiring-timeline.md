# Uncle May's Flagship Store - Hiring Timeline
## From Funding Close to Store Opening

**Purpose:** Map the critical path for hiring 11 employees before store opening  
**Assumption:** 12-16 week timeline from funding close to store opening  
**Risk buffer:** All hires target 1 week early to accommodate delays

---

## Timeline Overview

```
Funding Close (Week 0) → Store Opening (Week 16)

Critical Path:
- Weeks 0-2:    GM onboarding, department lead recruiting launched
- Weeks 3-6:    Department leads hired and onboarded
- Weeks 7-10:   Assistant Manager + Stock Lead hired
- Weeks 11-14:  Cashiers and Stock Associates hired and trained
- Week 15:      Full team training and soft opening prep
- Week 16:      STORE OPENING
```

## Risk Mitigation Strategies

### Risk 1: Funding Delay
**Scenario:** Funding takes 2-4 extra weeks to close  
**Mitigation:**
- Hold GM start until funding is confirmed (keep Matt Weschler warm with monthly check-ins)
- Delay all recruiting until $400K SAFE minimum is secured
- Keep JDs and hiring materials ready to launch immediately

---

### Risk 2: Key Hire Declines Offer
**Scenario:** Top candidate for Department Lead or Assistant Manager declines  
**Mitigation:**
- Always have 2-3 backup candidates in pipeline for leadership roles
- Move to backup within 48 hours of decline
- Extend timeline by 1 week if needed, but protect cashier training window

---

### Risk 3: Low Application Volume for Hourly Roles
**Scenario:** <20 applications for cashier or stock roles  
**Mitigation:**
- Increase Indeed sponsorship budget by $100-200
- Host in-person hiring event at community center (walk-in interviews)
- Offer sign-on bonus ($100-200 for cashiers who complete 90 days)
- Leverage Uncle May's mission to attract purpose-driven candidates

---

### Risk 4: Store Build-Out Delays
**Scenario:** Construction runs 2-4 weeks late  
**Mitigation:**
- Build 2-week buffer into hiring timeline (hire cashiers Week 13 even if store opens Week 18)
- Keep hourly staff engaged with paid pre-opening tasks (cleaning, stocking, training)
- Use delay time for extended training and team-building

---

### Risk 5: High Early Turnover (Cashiers/Stock)
**Scenario:** 1-2 cashiers quit in first 30 days  
**Mitigation:**
- Hire 1 extra cashier (6 instead of 5) as buffer
- Maintain active pipeline of backup candidates for 60 days post-opening
- Conduct 30-day check-ins with all hourly staff to address issues early
