- **Cashiers (4-6):** $200-400 total (local boost, 10-14 days, high volume expected)
- **Stock Associates (1-2):** $100-150 total (local boost, 7-10 days)

**When to Sponsor:**
- Leadership roles (Dept Leads, Asst Manager): Sponsor immediately to maximize visibility
- Hourly roles (Cashiers, Stock): Start organic, sponsor if <20 applications in 48 hours

**How Sponsorship Works:**
- Indeed charges per-click (not per-application)
- Average cost: $1-5 per click depending on role and location
- Budget controls ensure you don't overspend
- Pause sponsorship anytime if application volume is sufficient

## Application Management

### Target Application Volume (by role)

| Role | Target Applications | Timeline |
|------|---------------------|----------|
| Department Leads | 10-15 per role | 2 weeks |
| Assistant Manager | 8-12 | 3 weeks |
| Receiving/Stock Lead | 8-12 | 3 weeks |
| Cashiers | 30-40 total | 4 weeks |
| Stock Associates | 20-30 total | 4 weeks |

### Application Screening Criteria

**Red Flags (auto-reject):**
- No grocery or retail experience (for leadership roles)
- Unprofessional resume (typos, poor formatting, generic)
- Unrealistic salary expectations (e.g., $80K for cashier)
- Unable to work required schedule (weekends, evenings)

**Green Flags (priority review):**
- Relevant experience at Whole Foods, Mariano's, Trader Joe's, Pete's Fresh Market
- Chicago South Side resident (Hyde Park, Woodlawn, Bronzeville, Kenwood)
- Explicitly mentions Uncle May's mission in cover letter
- Longevity at previous employers (2+ years same role)
- Referrals from Matt Weschler's network

### Interview Funnel

**Leadership Roles (Department Leads, Assistant Manager, Stock Lead):**
1. Application review → Top 10-15 candidates
2. Phone screen (15 min) → Top 6-8 candidates
3. In-person interview (45-60 min, GM + CEO panel) → Top 3-4 candidates
4. Reference checks → Top 2 candidates
5. Offer extended to #1, #2 as backup

**Hourly Roles (Cashiers, Stock Associates):**
1. Application review → Top 30-40 candidates
2. Group interview (8-10 candidates per session, 45 min) → Top 12-15 candidates
3. Individual follow-up interview (15-20 min) → Top 7-10 candidates
4. Reference checks → Top 5-7 candidates
5. Offers extended (with 1-2 backups)
