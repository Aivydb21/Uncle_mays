## Grant Opportunities

### USDA FINI (Food Insecurity Nutrition Incentive)
- **Program:** Competitive grants for SNAP incentive programs (cash incentives for fresh produce purchases)
- **Eligible Applicants:** Nonprofits, government agencies, farmers markets, state associations
  - **For-profit retailers:** Can participate but CANNOT be lead organization (must partner with nonprofit/government)
- **Match Requirement:** $1:$1 match (cash or in-kind from state/local government or private sources)
- **Uncle May's Angle:** Partner with nonprofit (e.g., Hyde Park Kenwood Food Pantry) as SNAP retailer, they apply for grant, we supply produce
- **Next Action:** Identify nonprofit partner, discuss FINI grant application for SNAP incentive pilot

**Sources:**
- [FINI Program - Farmers Market Coalition](https://farmersmarketcoalition.org/advocacy/fini/)
- [SNAP Farmer Producer Grants](https://www.fns.usda.gov/snap/farmer-producer/farmers-market/grant)

### WIC Farmers Market Nutrition Program (FMNP)
- **Program:** Provides fresh, locally grown fruits and vegetables to WIC participants
- **Eligible Vendors:** Farmers markets, farm stands, roadside stands, community supported agriculture (CSA) programs
- **Uncle May's Angle:** Flagship store could qualify as WIC-authorized vendor for FMNP vouchers
- **Next Action:** Research WIC vendor authorization process for Illinois, identify FMNP coordinator

**Sources:**
- [WIC FMNP](https://www.fns.usda.gov/fmnp/wic-farmers-market-nutrition-program)

### Senior Farmers Market Nutrition Program
- **Program:** Low-income seniors receive vouchers for fresh fruits, vegetables, honey, herbs
- **Eligible Vendors:** Farmers markets, roadside stands, community supported agriculture (CSA) programs
- **Uncle May's Angle:** Similar to WIC FMNP, flagship could accept senior vouchers
- **Next Action:** Research Illinois senior FMNP program, vendor authorization
