## Personalization Checklist (Before Sending)

For each email, customize:
- [ ] **Recipient name and title** (research on LinkedIn, org website)
- [ ] **Organization-specific detail** (sustainability commitment, patient care mission, event types, etc.)
- [ ] **Specific offering** (dining halls vs. catering vs. office perks vs. nonprofit donation)
- [ ] **Mutual connection or warm intro** (if available—check Anthony's network first)
- [ ] **Timeline context** (are they in budget cycle, RFP season, event planning?)
- [ ] **Subject line** (make it specific to their org, not generic)

---

## Outreach Guardrails

- **No volume commitments** without COO approval on supply capacity
- **No pricing quotes** without CFO coordination on margins
- **No contracts >$10K or >6 months** without CEO sign-off
- **Always emphasize:** Local partnership, data-driven platform, mission alignment (not just "we sell produce")
- **Flagship not open yet:** Acknowledge timing ("launching this year"), pivot to online operations or post-opening pilot
