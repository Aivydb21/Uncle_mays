### 3. Key Findings

#### Quick Wins
- **Cook County Bonfire:** Can register and activate immediately (30-45 min)
- **City of Chicago iSupplier:** 3-day processing after application submitted (30-45 min application time)

#### Complex Requirements
- **Illinois IPG:** Requires 3 separate IL registrations BEFORE vendor registration:
  1. IL Secretary of State business registration
  2. IL State Board of Elections registration number
  3. IDHR Public Contract number (if 15+ employees)

#### CPS Different Model
- **Chicago Public Schools:** Cannot register directly as vendor
- Two paths only:
  1. Win a public RFP/RFQ for food service
  2. Get a school principal to sponsor/request Uncle May's as vendor
- Strategy: Monitor CPS procurement opportunities + build relationships with Hyde Park school principals

#### Required Documents (For ALL Portals)
1. Federal Employer Identification Number (EIN)
2. IRS Form W-9 (completed and signed)
3. SAM.gov Unique Entity Identifier (UEI)
4. SAM.gov CAGE Code
5. Business insurance certificates (general liability, workers compensation)
6. Illinois business license or Certificate of Good Standing
7. Banking information (for ACH payment setup)
8. Illinois Secretary of State registration number (for IPG)
9. Illinois State Board of Elections registration number (for IPG)
