## State Registrations (Illinois)

### Illinois Procurement Gateway (IPG)
- **Status:** ❓ UNKNOWN (READY TO REGISTER)
- **Registration Type:** State vendor portal for Illinois procurement opportunities
- **Portal:** [Illinois Procurement Gateway](https://ipg.vendorreg.com/)
- **Cost:** FREE (no cost to register)
- **Requirements:** 
  - Federal EIN or SSN
  - Illinois Department of Human Rights (IDHR) Public Contract # (required if 15+ employees)
  - Illinois State Board of Elections Registration Number
  - Authorization to do business in Illinois from IL Secretary of State
- **Process:** Submit company info electronically, Chief Procurement Office reviews/approves, vendor receives IPG registration number
- **e-Bidding:** Requires Illinois Procurement Bulletin account + IPG registration number in good standing
- **Pathway to Procurement:** [pathway2procurement.illinois.gov](https://pathway2procurement.illinois.gov/)
- **Bid Alerts:** Email notifications for selected business codes
- **Next Action:** Gather required registration numbers (IDHR, State Board of Elections), register on IPG, obtain IPG registration number for e-bidding

- **Contact:** Commission on Equity and Inclusion (CEI), 115 S. LaSalle St Suite 4N, Chicago IL 60603, (312) 814-4190, CEI.BEP@illinois.gov
