## Program #1: 8(a) Business Development (HIGHEST PRIORITY)

### Eligibility Requirements
- **Ownership:** 51%+ owned and controlled by U.S. citizens who are socially and economically disadvantaged
- **Social Disadvantage:** Presumed for Black Americans (no narrative required if owner identifies as Black)
- **Economic Disadvantage:**
  - Personal net worth < $850K (excluding primary residence and business ownership)
  - Adjusted gross income < $400K (average over last 3 years)
  - Total assets < $6.5M
- **Program Duration:** 9 years of eligibility
- **Certification:** SBA application process (6-12 months)

### Strategic Value for Uncle May's
- **Contract Access:** $72B+ annually in sole-source and competitive 8(a) set-asides
- **USDA Fit:** USDA has significant 8(a) set-asides for produce procurement, food service, nutrition programs
- **Sole-Source Authority:** Contracts up to $4.5M (goods) or $7M (services) can be awarded sole-source to 8(a) firms
- **Business Development Support:** SBA provides mentorship, training, and technical assistance

## Program #2: WOSB (Women-Owned Small Business)

### Eligibility Requirements
- **Ownership:** 51%+ owned and controlled by women who are U.S. citizens
- **Control:** Women manage day-to-day operations AND make long-term decisions
- **NAICS Codes:** Must operate in approved NAICS codes (700+ eligible, includes food retail/wholesale)
- **Certification:** SBA Certify or third-party certifier (WBENC, NWBOC, etc.)

### Strategic Value for Uncle May's
- **Contract Access:** $26B+ annually in WOSB set-asides
- **USDA Fit:** USDA has WOSB set-asides for food service and procurement
- **Federal + State:** WOSB certification also opens state-level women-owned set-asides (Illinois, Chicago)

### Uncle May's Qualification (Requires CEO Confirmation)
- **Ownership:** Is Uncle May's 51%+ owned by a woman/women? (Confirm: _____)
- **Control:** Do women manage day-to-day operations and make long-term decisions? (Confirm: _____)
- **NAICS:** Food retail (NAICS 445) and wholesale (NAICS 424) are eligible ✓

**Recommendation:** If Uncle May's is women-owned, **APPLY FOR WOSB**. Fast certification (30-90 days via third-party certifier) and strong strategic value for food contracts.

## Program #3: SDVOSB (Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned)

### Eligibility Requirements
- **Ownership:** 51%+ owned and controlled by service-disabled veterans
- **Disability:** Any VA-rated service-connected disability (0-100%)
- **Certification:** SBA VetCert

### Strategic Value
- **Contract Access:** $24B+ annually in SDVOSB set-asides
- **Sole-Source Authority:** Strong sole-source preferences

### Uncle May's Qualification (Requires CEO Confirmation)
- **Ownership:** Is Anthony Ivy or any co-owner a service-disabled veteran? (Confirm: _____)

**Recommendation:** If owner is service-disabled veteran, **APPLY FOR SDVOSB**. This is a highly valuable set-aside with strong federal contracting preferences.

## Program #4: HUBZone (Historically Underutilized Business Zone)

### Eligibility Requirements
- **Office Location:** Principal office must be in a HUBZone
- **Employee Residency:** 35% of employees must live in a HUBZone
- **Ownership:** 51%+ owned by U.S. citizens
- **Certification:** SBA HUBZone application

### Strategic Value
- **Contract Access:** $9B+ annually in HUBZone set-asides
- **10% Price Preference:** HUBZone firms get 10% price evaluation preference in full-and-open competitions

### Hyde Park HUBZone Status (Requires Verification)
- **Check:** Is Uncle May's flagship address (Hyde Park) in a HUBZone?
- **Tool:** [SBA HUBZone Map](https://maps.certify.sba.gov/hubzone/map)
- **Employee Residency:** Do 35%+ of employees live in Hyde Park or other HUBZone areas?

**Recommendation:** Check HUBZone map for Hyde Park address. If qualified, HUBZone offers strong price preference but has lower contract volume than 8(a) or WOSB. **Pursue only if 8(a)/WOSB don't apply.**
