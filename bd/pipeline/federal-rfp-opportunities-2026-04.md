## STRATEGIC REGISTRATION PATHWAYS (Set Up Now, Bid Later)

### 4. USDA Foods Approved Vendor Program (WBSCM)
**Status:** Open enrollment  
**Our Fit:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ EXCELLENT

**What It Is:**
- Approved vendors can bid on all USDA Foods solicitations (200+ commodity types)
- Access to National School Lunch Program, WIC, SNAP, emergency food programs
- All bidding done through WBSCM (Web-Based Supply Chain Management) system

**Why Register Now:**
- **Pre-qualification for all USDA purchases** - once approved, can bid on any solicitation
- **No cost to register** - just paperwork and verification
- **Opens Section 32 + all USDA Foods programs**

**Registration Process:**
1. Contact AMS New Vendor coordinator at WBSCMAMSHelpdesk@usda.gov
2. Complete WBSCM Vendor Registration Form
3. Provide PACA license, SAM registration, financial docs
4. AMS Contracting Officer reviews and approves
5. Receive WBSCM Corporate Vendor Administrator role

**Timeline:** 30-60 days

**Next Step:** **IMMEDIATE ACTION** - Email AMS help desk to request vendor registration package

### 5. GSA Multiple Award Schedule (MAS) - Food Service Category
**Status:** Open enrollment (formerly GSA Schedule 73)  
**Our Fit:** ⭐⭐⭐ MEDIUM (high effort, long-term value)

**What It Is:**
- GSA Schedule allows pre-approved vendors to sell to all federal agencies without competing bids
- Food service category covers produce, food distribution, hospitality services
- Once approved, any federal agency can purchase directly through GSA eBuy

**Why This Matters:**
- **Streamlined federal sales** - agencies can buy from us without RFP process
- **Nationwide reach** - not limited to specific contracts or regions
- **5-year contract** with option to renew
- **Credibility boost** - "GSA Schedule holder" status attracts agencies

**Challenges:**
- **Complex application** - extensive pricing, compliance, financial documentation
- **Audit requirements** - annual reporting and price verification
- **Best pricing commitment** - must offer federal government your best commercial rates

**Timeline:** 6-12 months application process

**Next Step:** Research GSA MAS requirements and decide if worth the investment (defer until Year 2)
