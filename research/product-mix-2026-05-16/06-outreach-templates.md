# Wave A vendor outreach templates

**Prepared:** 2026-05-17
**Owner:** Anthony + Zoe + Claude
**Use:** First-contact email to vendors in the Suppliers DB (`appm6F6H9obydzAM2 / Suppliers`).

## Operating notes

- Send from `anthony@unclemays.com`. Reply-to the same; Anthony handles responses personally.
- One template per category, swap the bracketed `[ITEMS]` and `[VENDOR_NAME]` lines.
- No Calendly link in the first touch. If they respond positively, second email proposes a 15-min call.
- Max 10 outreach emails per day to keep replies manageable.
- Track each send in the Suppliers table: add a `Last contacted` date column if not already present, set status to "contacted" so we do not double-touch.
- Soft 7-day re-touch on no-reply; one nudge max, then move on.

## Voice and rules

- Founder-to-founder, not corporate. Anthony is a Chicago Booth MBA and the CEO. Tone is direct, respectful, and operator-aware. Vendors are running real businesses with thin margins; respect their time.
- Lead with what we offer them (a paying retail channel in Chicago), not with what we want.
- Be specific about volume and cadence so they can quickly say yes / no / counter.
- No em dashes. Use commas, colons, or periods. (Standing CEO rule.)
- No "synergy", "circle back", "love to chat", "exciting opportunity". Plain words.
- Keep total length under 200 words. Vendors do not finish long emails.

## Reply triage

Anthony reads replies personally. Standard responses:

- **Yes, full info provided:** schedule a 15-minute call, set a first PO in writing.
- **Yes, partial info:** reply asking for the missing pieces.
- **Maybe, asks for more info:** send a one-pager (Uncle May's brand promise, current SKU count, delivery footprint, projected first-year volume). The one-pager does not exist yet; flag for Zoe to draft.
- **No, too small / too big / not the right fit:** thank them, ask for a referral to another vendor in their network. Add a note to the Suppliers row.
- **No reply after 7 days:** one polite nudge with a single-sentence reminder. After 14 days, no further follow-up; mark "no response" in the Suppliers row.
