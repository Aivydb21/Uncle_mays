Preferred Equity Terms

$400K minimum tranche, approximately 16% fully-diluted ownership. 8 to 10% accruing preferred return, non-mandatory cash pay. Distributable cash flow swept to investors until cumulative realized cash return of 3.0 to 3.2x invested capital; sweep terminates at threshold and residual equity continues pro rata. No participating preferred. No ratchets. No conversion mechanics. Designed for operator-investors who want simple, transparent alignment.

Why the Asset Underwrites

  • Existing $8M+ revenue location, not greenfield. Refrigeration, mechanical, and infrastructure already in place.
  • 40% currently utilized; meaningful selling-area expansion within the existing footprint.
  • Hyde Park demographic is underserved by culturally aligned grocery; transit-connected with high foot traffic.
  • Margin expansion is sourcing-driven (COGS compresses 70.5% to 64.9%), not pricing.
  • Revenue held flat from Year 4 onward in the base case (no aggressive same-store assumption).
