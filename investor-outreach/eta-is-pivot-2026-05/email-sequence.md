## Notes on personalization

When sending, personalize the opener of email 1 only, with one of:

- A reference to a specific deal the LP has backed that matches the pattern (single-asset, SBA-leveraged, operator-led).
- A reference to the LP's stated thesis on self-funded searchers if it is on their site.
- A Polsky / Booth / Wharton / HBS connection if real.

Do not personalize emails 2 through 4. Personalization on follow-ups reads as inauthentic at this end of the market.

## Stop conditions

- Stop on any reply.
- Pause on OOO and resume on the LP's return date.
- Bounce killswitch at 5 percent for the campaign.
- Do not send emails 2 through 4 if email 1 bounced or was suppressed.
