## Methodology v2

**Geographic** (max 5): Chicago = 5, Midwest = 4, US other = 2, international = 0.

**Title text:** self-funded language = +5, search investor language = +4, named ETA firm = +3, independent sponsor = +3, holdco/family office/operator = +2, **searcher / solo-searcher (operator on the wrong side of the table) = -3** (new), too-big PE shop = -4, wrong sector = -3.

**Alumni** (new): Booth = +3, UChicago non-Booth = +2, HBS = +2.

**Wave 1 = score >= 7, OR Chicago with any positive signal, OR Booth alum with any positive signal.** All exclude disqualifiers.

**Wave 2 = US-based, score 4 to 6, has positive signal, not in Wave 1.**

**Filters NOT applied** (data not in Searchfunder paste):

- Stated check size ($50K to $250K)
- Stated sector preference (food / retail / grocery)
- Email or contact path
