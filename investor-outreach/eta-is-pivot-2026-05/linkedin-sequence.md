# LinkedIn Sequence, Self-Funded Searcher LPs and FOs

**Sender:** Anthony Ivy (personal LinkedIn)
**Audience:** ETA / self-funded searcher LPs, family offices that back self-funded searchers
**Cadence:** profile view, connection request, DM 1 on accept, DM 2 at +5 days, optional InMail at +12 days
**Style rules:** No em dashes. Investor-to-investor tone. No links in the connection request (LinkedIn flags). Phone number reserved for after acceptance.

---

## Step 1, Profile view (Day 0)

Just view the profile. No engagement. Lets the LP see Anthony's profile in their "Who viewed your profile" feed before the connection request lands. No copy needed.

---

## Step 2, Connection request (Day 1)

LinkedIn caps connection-request notes at 300 characters. Three variants depending on the relationship surface area:

**Variant A, generic self-funded SF audience:**

> {{first_name}}, running a self-funded searcher deal on a single Chicago grocery asset, $400K preferred + $2M SBA 7(a). 18.4% / 23.3% IRR base case, 10-year hold. Connecting in case it fits your sleeve. Anthony

**Variant B, LP with public ETA / SF thesis on their profile:**

> {{first_name}}, saw your work on self-funded searcher deals. Running one of my own, single Chicago grocery asset with SBA 7(a) + preferred equity. Connecting and would value the read when timing fits. Anthony

**Variant C, Booth / Polsky / mutual connection:**

> {{first_name}}, fellow {{Booth / HBS / etc.}}, running a self-funded searcher deal on a Chicago grocery asset. SBA + preferred. Polsky referral on the deck. Connecting to compare notes. Anthony

Pick the variant per contact. Do not blast Variant A across all of them.

---

## Stop conditions

- Stop the entire sequence on any reply.
- Do not send DM 2 if the LP has posted within the last 24 hours about a personal life event (sympathy / promotion / etc.).
- Do not InMail outside of business hours US Central.

## Posting cadence

In parallel with the LinkedIn outreach, Anthony posts twice per week from the personal account on:

- The deal pattern (self-funded searcher, SBA + preferred, single-asset operator-led)
- The asset (existing $8M+ Chicago grocery location, named GM, transit-connected)
- The operator angle (Booth, prior PE / M&A, why this category, why this market)

Keep posts general. Do not name individual LPs publicly. Confidentiality on the data room and the pitchbook stays intact.

## Volume limits

- Maximum 10 connection requests per day from the personal account
- Maximum 5 DMs per day to newly accepted connections
- Maximum 2 InMails per week (premium budget conservation)

## Tracking

LinkedIn outreach status logged in the existing investor-CRM tracker, same fields as the email pipeline. Status values: viewed, connected, DM1 sent, DM1 read, DM2 sent, DM2 read, InMail sent, replied, declined, archived.
