# Uncle May's Produce — A Market for Black Growers and Makers

**Store 1 of the first data and distribution platform for Black food consumption. Built to put Black producers on the shelf, into distribution, and in front of real demand.**

CONFIDENTIAL | Prepared for Potlikker Capital | June 2026

Potlikker funds the producers. Uncle May's gives them the market. Together that is capital plus market access, the combination that lets growers and makers reach buyers, get paid, and keep value in the community.

## What Your Producers Get
- **Shelf and distribution:** a real, established retail outlet and distribution channel in Hyde Park, a high-income, high-intent Black market with more than $8M in proven annual revenue.
- **Demand data:** we show producers what sells, where, and when, so they plan production on signal instead of guesswork.
- **Fair terms:** terms designed to keep value with the grower, so ownership and margin stay with the people doing the work.
- **Room to grow:** this is Store 1 of a planned 82-store rollout, so the shelf expands as we do.

## How Onboarding Works
A simple path from producer to shelf: introduction and vetting, listing with cost and retail, placement in the store and distribution, then an ongoing demand feedback loop so producers see how their products move. We are building this pipeline now and have room for Potlikker's producers in the first cohorts.

## Why It Fits Potlikker
Potlikker's mission is market access and community ownership for socially and economically disadvantaged producers. That is exactly what our platform delivers. Your capital helps producers build capacity. Our shelf, distribution, and data give them somewhere to sell and the signal to grow. Value circulates in the community, which is the whole point.
