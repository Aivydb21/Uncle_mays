## The Opportunity

**The gap**

- 67% of Black shoppers visit 3+ stores monthly (Progressive Grocer)
- 40% say their store doesn't carry their cultural foods (McKinsey)
- Stores in Black neighborhoods stock 52% fewer produce varieties (USDA)
- No system captures Black food demand at the SKU level

**The model**

- **Retail.** Curated 4,000 SKU format, 7,500-12,000 sq ft, neighborhood scale
- **Vendor.** Direct sourcing through a Black-farmer and Black-CPG network
- **Data.** SKU-level demand, pricing, and behavior aggregated across stores and the produce-box channel
- **Comparable.** Northgate Market (Hispanic-grocery analog) sold to a KKR-backed buyer in 2024 at ~$1B+. Trader Joe's, the curated-format benchmark, generates ~$2,200/sq ft.
