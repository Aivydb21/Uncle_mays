### Status Key
- **Prospect** - Identified, not yet contacted
- **Contacted** - Initial outreach sent (via Apollo or direct)
- **Meeting** - Meeting scheduled or completed
- **DD** - In due diligence / reviewing materials
- **Committed** - Verbal or written commitment
- **Closed** - SAFE signed and funded
- **Pass** - Replied with decline; no further automated outreach

### LI Status Key
- **--** - No LinkedIn action taken
- **Viewed** - Profile viewed
- **Requested** - Connection request sent
- **Connected** - Connection accepted
- **DM1 Sent** - First DM sent (Day 7)
- **DM2 Sent** - Second DM sent (Day 14)
- **InMail Sent** - InMail sent (Day 22, non-connected only)
- **LI Reply** - Replied via LinkedIn

- **Family** - Family office
