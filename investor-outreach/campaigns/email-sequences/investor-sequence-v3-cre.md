## Sequence Notes (v3-cre Specific)

**Voice:** Brand voice ("we" / "our"), referencing Anthony by name as founder/CEO. Never first-person "I" from Anthony.

**From-name:** "Uncle May's Produce" on investmentrelations@unclemays.com.

**Audience tailoring vs v2-tier2:**
- Email 1 leads with the physical asset (10,000 sq ft, commercial lease, Hyde Park) to resonate with CRE professionals
- Email 2 emphasizes the capital stack structure and institutional-grade unit economics
- Emails 3-4 are similar to v2-tier2 (data moat and close), since the platform story appeals to all investor types
- No changes to CTA pattern or style rules

**CTA pattern:**
- Email 1: "Worth a conversation?" (low friction, reply-based)
- Email 2: "Happy to share the deck or financial model" (offer value)
- Email 3: "We'd welcome 20 minutes" (specific but not presumptuous)
- Email 4: "If this doesn't fit, no worries" (permission to say no)

**Attachments:** Only attach teaser on Email 1. Offer deck/model in Emails 2-3 as a reply trigger.

**Calendly:** Do NOT include in cold emails. Send scheduling link only after someone replies.

**Style rules:** No em dashes (use commas, periods, or colons). Max two paragraphs per email body. No Calendly in cold outreach. No "food desert" framing.

**Signature:** Full signature with phone + website on Emails 1 and 4 only. Shorter sign-off on Emails 2 and 3.
