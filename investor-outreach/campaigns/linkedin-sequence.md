## Connection Request Templates (160-char limit)

### Template A: Shared Background
> Anthony Ivy, Booth MBA. Building the first data platform for Black food consumption. Your work at {{Firm}} caught my attention. Would value connecting.

### Template B: Sector Relevance
> Anthony Ivy, Chicago Booth. Raising a seed round in food infrastructure. {{Firm}}'s thesis in {{sector}} aligns closely. Glad to connect.

### Template C: Chicago/Geo
> Anthony Ivy, Booth MBA. Building grocery infrastructure in Hyde Park. Fellow Chicago investor. Would like to connect.

**Selection guide:**
- Template A: Default for most contacts
- Template B: When the firm has a clear sector focus (food, consumer, impact)
- Template C: Chicago-based investors only

## Style Rules (Same as Email)

- No em dashes (use commas, periods, or colons)
- Max two short paragraphs per message
- Investor-to-investor tone leveraging Anthony's Booth MBA, PE, M&A background
- No Calendly in cold outreach (send after reply)
- Permission to opt out increases responses ("no pressure", "totally understand")
- Never attach files via LinkedIn DM. Offer to send.
- Private DMs can reference team credentials and location. Public posts cannot.

## Daily Workflow (15-20 min, single morning block)

1. **Check Sales Navigator alerts** (2 min) - Tier 1 contacts who posted? Quick comment.
2. **Send 10 connection requests** for the next batch (5 min) - templates A/B/C, slight personalization
3. **View profiles** for the batch after that (2 min)
4. **Send 1-2 DMs** to Day 7 or Day 14 contacts who are connected (3 min)
5. **Send 1 InMail** if any Day 22 non-connected non-responders are queued (2 min)
6. **2x/week:** draft and schedule a LinkedIn post (5 min on post days)

### Weekly Totals

| Action | Volume |
|--------|--------|

| Profile views | ~50 |

| InMails | ~3-5 |

## Automation Policy

**Do NOT automate LinkedIn actions.** Account ban risk is catastrophic for a CEO fundraising.

**Safe:**
- LinkedIn's native post scheduler
- Sales Navigator saved searches and alerts
- CSV export from Sales Navigator for CRM tracking
- Claude updating investor-crm.md when Anthony reports activity

**Banned:**
- Dux-Soup, Linked Helper, PhantomBuster, Octopus CRM
- API scraping tools
- Any third-party tool that logs into LinkedIn
