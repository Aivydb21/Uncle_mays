**Status:** NOT DETERMINED

**Current Approach:** Apollo + LinkedIn outreach suggests 506(b) (warm leads, no public advertising). Confirm this with CEO.  
**Action:** CEO to confirm 506(b) approach OR switch to 506(c) if we want to advertise
