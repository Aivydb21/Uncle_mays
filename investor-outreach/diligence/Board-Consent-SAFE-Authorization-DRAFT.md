# UNANIMOUS WRITTEN CONSENT OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS  
# OF UNCLE MAY'S PRODUCE, INC.

**Date:** [To Be Filled]

---

The undersigned, constituting all of the members of the Board of Directors (the "Board") of **Uncle May's Produce, Inc.**, a Delaware corporation (the "Company"), pursuant to Section 141(f) of the Delaware General Corporation Law, hereby adopt the following resolutions without a meeting:

---

## AUTHORIZATION OF SAFE FINANCING

**WHEREAS**, the Company desires to raise capital through the issuance of Simple Agreements for Future Equity ("SAFEs") to accredited investors;

**WHEREAS**, the Board has reviewed the terms of the proposed SAFE financing and believes that such financing is in the best interests of the Company and its stockholders;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT:**

### RESOLVED, that the Board hereby authorizes and approves the issuance and sale of SAFEs to accredited investors in one or more closings on the following terms:

- **Form of Agreement:** Post-Money Simple Agreement for Future Equity, substantially in the form attached hereto as **Exhibit A** (the "SAFE")
- **Post-Money Valuation Cap:** $5,000,000
- **Discount Rate:** 20%
- **Minimum Investment per Investor:** $25,000
- **Maximum Aggregate Amount:** $750,000
- **Target Minimum to Trigger SBA Facility:** $400,000

### FURTHER RESOLVED, that the officers of the Company, and each of them individually, are hereby authorized and directed to:

1. Execute and deliver SAFEs on behalf of the Company to accredited investors on the terms set forth above, with such changes, insertions, or omissions as such officer may approve (such approval to be conclusively evidenced by the execution and delivery thereof);

2. Negotiate and accept subscriptions for SAFEs from accredited investors, subject to the terms approved herein;

3. Take all actions necessary or advisable to verify that each investor is an "accredited investor" within the meaning of Rule 501 of Regulation D under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended (the "Securities Act");

4. Accept payment from investors for the SAFEs and deposit such funds in the Company's bank account(s);

5. Issue SAFEs to investors upon receipt of payment;

6. File Form D with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission pursuant to Rule 503 of Regulation D under the Securities Act within fifteen (15) days after the first sale of a SAFE;

7. File any required state securities notices or applications in states where investors are located, as may be required under applicable state securities laws;

8. Update the Company's capitalization table to reflect the issuance of SAFEs;

9. Engage legal counsel as necessary to ensure compliance with federal and state securities laws in connection with the SAFE financing; and

10. Execute and deliver such other documents and take such other actions as they deem necessary, advisable, or appropriate to carry out the intent and accomplish the purposes of the foregoing resolutions.

### FURTHER RESOLVED, that all actions heretofore taken by any officer or director of the Company in connection with the matters contemplated by the foregoing resolutions are hereby ratified, confirmed, and approved in all respects.

### FURTHER RESOLVED, that the officers of the Company are hereby authorized to prepare and deliver to the investors and potential investors such disclosure materials, financial statements, business plans, and other information concerning the Company as such officers deem appropriate, subject to confidentiality agreements as deemed necessary.

---

## EXEMPTION FROM REGISTRATION

**WHEREAS**, the Board has determined that the offer and sale of the SAFEs will be made in reliance on the exemption from registration provided by Section 4(a)(2) of the Securities Act and Rule 506(b) of Regulation D promulgated thereunder;

**RESOLVED**, that the Board hereby finds and determines that:

1. The offer and sale of the SAFEs shall be made only to "accredited investors" as defined in Rule 501(a) of Regulation D;

2. The offer and sale of the SAFEs shall be made without general solicitation or general advertising, and shall be made only to investors with whom the Company or its representatives have a pre-existing, substantive relationship or who are otherwise contacted through means that do not constitute general solicitation;

3. Each investor shall receive or have access to such information concerning the Company as such investor requests or deems material, and each investor shall have the opportunity to ask questions of, and receive answers from, the Company's management concerning the Company and the terms and conditions of the SAFE financing;

4. Each investor shall represent in writing that:
   - The investor is purchasing the SAFE for the investor's own account for investment purposes only and not with a view to, or for resale in connection with, any distribution thereof;
   - The investor is an accredited investor;
   - The investor has such knowledge and experience in financial and business matters that the investor is capable of evaluating the merits and risks of the investment in the SAFE; and
   - The investor can afford a complete loss of the investment in the SAFE.

5. The certificates or instruments evidencing the SAFEs (if any) shall bear a legend restricting transfer in accordance with applicable federal and state securities laws.

---

## GENERAL AUTHORITY

**RESOLVED**, that the appropriate officers of the Company be, and each of them hereby is, authorized and empowered, in the name and on behalf of the Company, to execute and deliver, or cause to be executed and delivered, all such documents, agreements, certificates, notices, and other instruments, and to take or cause to be taken all such other actions, as such officer deems necessary, advisable, or appropriate to effectuate the purposes and intent of the foregoing resolutions, the taking of such actions and the execution and delivery of such documents to be conclusive evidence of the approval thereof by the Board.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that any and all actions previously taken by the officers and directors of the Company in furtherance of the purposes of the foregoing resolutions be, and each of them hereby is, ratified, confirmed, and approved.
