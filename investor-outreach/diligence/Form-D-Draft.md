**Narrative Explanation:**

Proceeds from this SAFE offering, combined with an SBA 7(a) loan ($2.007M conditionally approved from Busey Bank) and tenant improvement contributions ($100K), will fund the build-out and launch of Uncle May's Produce's flagship 10,000 sq ft grocery store in Hyde Park, Chicago.

The $400K minimum SAFE raise is required to trigger the SBA facility close. All SAFE proceeds will be used as equity capital to fund:
1. Store construction and equipment installation
2. Soft costs (architectural, engineering, permits, legal)
3. Initial inventory procurement
4. Pre-opening marketing and staffing
5. Working capital reserves for the first 6 months of operation

No proceeds will be used for executive compensation increases or shareholder distributions. All funds are allocated to capital expenditures and operating reserves for the flagship store launch.
