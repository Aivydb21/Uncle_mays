# S6 — Grants / Non-Dilutive / Accelerators

Grants, accelerators, non-dilutive capital, community financing, and competition capital. Entry style: application. No equity, no dilution — capital comes attached to program obligations (reporting, mentorship, cohort activity, etc.).

Activation gate: application window open; eligibility confirmed for Uncle May's (geography, revenue stage, sector, demographic eligibility).
