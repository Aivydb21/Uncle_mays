## Troubleshooting

**If ads are disapproved:**
- Check for policy violations in Ads Manager
- Most common: image text ratio >20% (shouldn't be an issue with our designs)
- Appeal if needed through Ads Manager

**If placements won't deselect:**
- Use "Advantage+ placements" toggle OFF first
- Then manually select only the placement you want

**If targeting is too narrow (<1,000 people):**
- Remove one interest at a time until audience is 10,000-50,000
- Keep location and age/gender targeting
