## iPad Setup Instructions

### Pre-Event (Apr 21-22)
1. **Transfer files to iPad:**
   - AirDrop all images from `public/images/` to iPad Photos app
   - Or: Email images to yourself and save to Photos
   - Organize into album: "1871 FoodTech Demo"

2. **Bookmark unclemays.com:**
   - Open Safari on iPad
   - Navigate to `https://unclemays.com`
   - Add to home screen (icon for quick access)
   - Test: Does it load quickly? Is it mobile-responsive?

3. **QR Code:**
   - Verify `qr-1871-showcase.png` links to correct UTM URL
   - If incorrect, generate new QR at qr-code-generator.com
   - Save corrected QR to Photos app

4. **Lead Capture Form:**
   - Once Google Form is created, add link to home screen
   - Name it: "1871 Leads"
   - Test form submission works on iPad

5. **Display Settings:**
   - Set screen timeout to "Never" (prevent auto-lock during event)
   - Brightness: 80-100% (table may have overhead lighting)
   - Do Not Disturb: ON (prevent notifications during demos)
   - Airplane mode: OFF (need WiFi/cellular for live site)

### Day-of Setup (Apr 23, 4:20 PM)
1. **Load hero image first:**
   - Open Photos app to "1871 FoodTech Demo" album
   - Display `hero-produce.jpg` as idle screen
   - Or: Open unclemays.com homepage in Safari (more dynamic)

2. **Quick-access setup:**
   - Swipe between Photos (product images) and Safari (live site)
   - Keep lead capture form link ready in Safari tab

3. **Demo flow:**
   - **Greeting:** Show hero-produce.jpg or unclemays.com homepage
   - **Product explanation:** Swipe to produce-box.jpg
   - **Brand story:** Swipe to heritage.jpg
   - **Live site demo:** Open unclemays.com in Safari, show shopping flow
   - **Lead capture:** Switch to "1871 Leads" form, hand iPad to prospect
