- Gap is not grocery access. It is **cultural sourcing + Black economic circulation**.
- 67% of Black shoppers visit 3+ stores monthly. 40% can't find cultural foods. 52% fewer produce varieties in Black neighborhoods.
- **There is no system of record for Black food consumption.** Vendors can't measure demand. Investors can't find the businesses. We are building the system. Retail = acquisition engine. Vendors = supply engine. Data = moat.

### 1:45 - 3:15 · What I've Actually Built (it's just me + the platform) ★
- **Say it explicitly: "Right now, it's me and the produce platform."**
- Flagship store has its own capital stack + own team (SBA debt, GM signed, architect engaged). **That's Chapter Two. Not what we're talking about today.**
- What's shipped solo:
  - Custom **Next.js** e-commerce, checkout, delivery routing, Stripe, address autocomplete. No Shopify.
  - Live catalog of Black-farmer-sourced products, launched 2026-04-30.
  - **Full observability**: LogRocket, Galileo AI, GA4, Meta Pixel + CAPI, daily Stripe / Mailchimp reporting. **Built without an agency.**
  - **34 paying customers** in first 3 weeks, **AOV ~$90** *(reconciliation in back pocket below)*.

### 3:15 - 5:00 · What The Customers Are Telling Us ★
- 5 deep customer interviews in early May.
- **Convergent signal: "Black farmers" is unprompted #1 driver in 4 of 5.** Beat price, beat speed, beat variety.
- **Most VCs expect price sensitivity in Black communities. Our data says the opposite.** Willingness-to-pay driver is **economic circulation**. That is the asset.
- **No-subscription is a TRUST signal**, not a convenience choice. Customers have been burned by hidden auto-renew terms in CSAs.
- **The honest gap — say it out loud:** "We are out of the decision set for a full grocery basket today. Produce alone doesn't replace a grocery trip. Our customers still go elsewhere for the rest of the cart. We know that. The top-30 SKU expansion is how we buy our way into the basket — scoped from what these same customers told us they're going elsewhere to buy."

### 5:00 - 6:30 · The Metrics And The Honest Gap ★★ (MOVED FORWARD)
- **Gate metrics I owe Fifth Star back:**
  - AOV $75-$100
  - GM ≥ 35%

### 7:30 - 7:45 · Why Now (close)

## Recording-day rules

- Quiet space. Stable connection. Notes are allowed (this sheet).
- Pace: ~150 wpm = ~1,150-word pitch fills ~7:45. **Don't fill the time** — Fifth Star explicitly said clear and concise is fine. Buffer is your friend.

- One number per claim. Don't stack stats.
- Pause between blocks. Helps them score against the rubric.
